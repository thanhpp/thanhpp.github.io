## Great life

- PERMA-V
    - Positive emotions
    - Engagement (what am I doing)
    - Relationships
    - Meaning (the world a better place)
    - Achievement
    - Vitality (healthy)

## Life Portfolio

- Strategic life area
    - Relationship
    - Body, mind, spirituality
    - Community & society
    - Job, learning & finances
    - Interests & entertainment
    - Personal care (daily living)
- Steps
    - Divide an average week avong the strategic life units
    - Rate the importane of each one
    - Rate the level of satisfaction with each one
- 2x2 matrix
    - Ox = Importance: high & low
    - Oy = Satisfaction: high & low
    - bubble size: time spent

## Portfolio choices

- Adjust the parameters
    - Importance
    - Satisfaction
    - Time spent

## Right portfolio

- Aligned to your dimensions
- Support your purpose & dimensions
- Increase time spent in **high important & high satisfaction**
