# Habit stacking

> https://youtu.be/OPw7HODLAyM

## Minimal habits

- Setting a commitment to do a paticular habit (but a lot **less than ideally**)
    - Example: not a full routine workout everyday -> 50 push-ups **everyday**
- smaller commitments
- To create a foundation/base line
- The hardest part: initialization
- Focus on the frenquency, don't break the chain
- GOAL: **maintain consistency**

## Overlapping tasks

- Stacking habits
- Example
    - Learning languages & reading
    - Listening & workout
- Strategies
    - spliting tasks: physical & cognitive/using mind => merge 1 and other
    - split by different cognitive mechanism
        - visual task
        - listen task

## Summarize

- Minimal habit: do low effort tasks to maintain consistency
- Overlapping: Reduce time by combining tasks
