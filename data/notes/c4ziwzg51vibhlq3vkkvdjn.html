Ideas

  • Walkthrough the topic top-down
    • Explain to yourself ~ teaching to another person

Method

  1. Choose a concept you want to learn about
  2. Explain it to a 12 year old (use simple words)
  3. Reflect, Refine, and Simplify
  4. Organize and Review
