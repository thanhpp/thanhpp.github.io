## User space

1. Create .fonts in the home directory
1. Move font files in to the ~/.fonts folder
1. Reload the fonts cache `sudo fc-cache -f -v`

## Share space

1. Create a folder in `/usr/local/share/fonts`
1. Move font files into that folder

## Recommend fonts

- Nerd fonts: https://www.nerdfonts.com/font-downloads
