Active & Passive task

  • Def
    • Active: Using a lot of your mind (creating, crarfting,...)
    • Passive: Don't use the mind too much (receiving information)
  • Active tasks are easier to focus
  • Key: Passive -> Active
    • Reading -> Taking notes

Concentration is a skill (can be improved)

  • Take a small focus set (20 mins) -> increase slowly
  • Take breaks
