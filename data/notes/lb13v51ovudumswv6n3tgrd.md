# Customer Insight through IDIs - Marketing Research

## Definitions

- IDIs: In-depth Interviews
    - conducting intensive interviewers with a number of respondents to explore their perspective
    - => group interviews, n interviewers - m respondents
- Marketing Research:
    - Primary Research methods
        - Interviews
        - Surveys
      - Primary vs Secondary (desk research) researchs  

## Question

- In-depth Interviews why & who:
    - small sample size
    - selective individuals
    - **valuable knowledge => Professional interview**
    - **Private interview => sensitive information => Need agreement**
    - Personalize data
    - **Difficulty to interview in a group**
- Where?
    - Interviewee preference
        - Schedule compatible
        - **Comfortable**
- How long?
    - <= 1 hours (45-60 mins) -> build questions
    - Agreements
    - < require time
        - Shrink time
        - move schedule (not recommend) -> to another day
        - Shift schedule (mins-hours) -> in day
        - Handle
            - questions preview send to interviewee
            - Ask high priority questions
